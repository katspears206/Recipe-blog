Mom's Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 1 large can of tomato sauce
  • 1 small can of tomato paste
  • Italian Spices
  • Crushed Red Pepper
  • Garlic Salt
  • Onion Powder
  • Oregano
  • Basil
  • One package of prefered spaghetti noodles

How to Make

First set a pot of water boiling. In a seperate pan pour the tomato
sauce and paste and stir until the paste is well mixed into the sauce. Spice
to taste. Boil the noodles for amount of time on the container. When the sauce
starts bubbling, place on low heat and stir occasionally until the noodles
are done. Once the noodles are done, strain them and mix in the sauce(either in
the larger pot or some other serving dish). Enjoy!

-Katlyn Spears 1/8/2016

Fancy Finnish Farfalle

Ingredients

  • 1 small jar of basil pesto
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 Green bell pepper
  • Cucumber
  • Mixed greens
  • 3 Roma tomatoes
  • One package of prefered farfalle(bowtie) noodles

How to Make

First set a pot of water boiling. While the water is boiling, cut all
of the vegetables(size however you like). In a large bowl put your mixed greens
and vegetables. Cut the lemon in half and sqeeze both sides into the bowl.
Mix the pesto into the greens and veggies(and lemon juics). Once the noodles are
done, dump them into the bowl as well and mix until the pasta is covred with
pesto. Serve and Enjoy!

Alfredo

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 1/3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/14 cups of grated fresh Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese
  • 2 tablespoons 1/3-less-fat cream cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley
  • Cracked black pepper
  • One package of prefered fettuccine noodles

How to Make

Melt butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Add garlic; cook 1
minute, stirring frequently. Stir in flour. Gradually add milk, stirring
with a whisk. Cook 6 minutes or until mixture thickens, stirring constantly. Add
1 cup Parmigiano-Reggiano, cream cheese, and salt, stirring with a whisk until
cheeses melt. Toss sauce with hot pasta. Sprinkle with remaining 1/4 cup
Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese and chopped parsley. Garnish with black pepper,
if desired. Serve immediately.

-Katlyn Spears 1/8/2016

Spinach Tomato Tortelini

Ingredients

  • 1 medium can of diced tomatoes with garlic and onion
  • 1 cup chopped fresh spinach
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • One package of cheese tortellini

How to Make

Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add the tortellini, and cook until
tender, about 10 minutes.While you get the tortellini going, combine the
tomatoes, spinach, salt, pepper, basil and garlic in a large saucepan over
medium heat. Cook and stir until the mixture begins to bubble.In a medium
bowl, whisk together the flour, milk and cream. Stir this mixture into the
saucepan along with the Parmesan cheese. Heat through, then reduce heat to
low, and simmer until thick, about 2 minutes.Drain the tortellini, but do not
rinse, then pour them into the saucepan with the sauce. Stir to coat, and serve.

Manicotti

Ingredients

  • 1 pint part-skim ricotta cheese
  • 8 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 (16oz) jar of spaghetti sauce
  • 5 1/2 ounces manicotti pasta

How to Make

Cook manicotti in boiling water until done. Drain, and rinse with cold
water.Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).In a large bowl, combine
ricotta, mozzarella, and 1/2 cup Parmesan, eggs, parsley, and salt and pepper.
Mix well.Pour 1/2 cup sauce into an 11x17 inch baking dish. Fill each manicotti
shell with 3 tablespoons cheese mixture, and arrange over sauce . Pour
remaining sauce over top, and sprinkle with remaining Parmesan cheese.Bake 45
minutes, or until bubbly.
